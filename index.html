About Ozon Detailing & Car Wash

Welcome to Ozon Detailing and Car Wash, where passion for cars meets precision and care. Founded on the principle that every vehicle deserves to look its absolute best, we have become a trusted name for discerning car and bike owners.

Our mission is simple: to provide an unparalleled detailing experience that restores, protects, and enhances your vehicle's appearance. We use only industry-leading products and the latest techniques, from meticulous hand washes to advanced ceramic coatings. Our skilled technicians treat every vehicle as their own, ensuring a flawless finish and complete customer satisfaction every time.

Our Services

Coating & Protection

Graphene Ceramic Coating

The pinnacle of paint protection. Graphene offers unmatched durability, slickness, and resistance to water spotting, keeping your car pristine for years.

Borophene Ceramic Coating

A thicker, more durable coating for superior chemical resistance and a deeper, candy-like gloss. Offers long-term protection for the serious enthusiast.

Ceramic Coating

Provides a high-gloss, hydrophobic shield against UV rays, bird droppings, and light scratches, making your vehicle easier to clean.

Paint Protection Film (PPF)

A transparent, self-healing film to protect your car's paint from rock chips, scratches, and road debris, preserving its value.

Underbody Coating

Protects your vehicle's chassis from rust, corrosion, and road debris with our durable, high-performance undercoating treatment.

Glass Coating

Improves visibility in rain by creating a super-hydrophobic surface. Makes glass easier to clean and reduces glare for safer driving.

Graphene Coating with Glass & Alloy Wheel Coating

Combines advanced graphene ceramic coating for paint protection with specialized coatings for glass and alloy wheels, ensuring all-around durability and shine.

Borophene Coating with Glass & Alloy Wheel Coating

Combines durable borophene ceramic coating with glass and alloy wheel coatings for superior protection, gloss, and easy maintenance across your vehicle.

Ceramic Coating with Glass Coating

Combines high-gloss ceramic coating for paint with hydrophobic glass coating, enhancing shine and improving visibility in all conditions.

Wash Packages

Premium Car Wash

A meticulous hand wash using pH-neutral shampoos and a gentle microfiber towel dry, ensuring every inch of your exterior is spotless.

Full Wash

Our complete package, including exterior body wash, interior vacuuming, and dashboard cleaning for a comprehensive clean.

Foam Wash

A gentle yet effective foam cannon wash that lifts away dirt and grime without harming your car's paintwork, followed by a careful hand dry.

Body Wash

A thorough exterior wash to remove daily dirt and grime, leaving your vehicle clean and refreshed.

Ceramic Wash

A special wash using SiO2-infused shampoos that boost your existing ceramic coating, enhancing gloss and water repellency.

Underbody Wash

A high-pressure wash to remove harmful salt, mud, and road grime from your vehicle's undercarriage, preventing rust and corrosion.

Engine Steam Wash

Safely and effectively clean your engine bay with steam, removing grease and dirt to improve performance and appearance.

Detailing, Restoration & Customization

Premium Polish

A multi-stage paint correction process that removes swirls and light scratches, restoring a deep, mirror-like shine to your car's paint.

Normal Polish

A single-stage polish that enhances gloss and clarity, effectively removing minor imperfections and revitalizing your car's finish.

Interior Cleaning

Deep cleaning of all interior surfaces, including vacuuming, upholstery shampooing, and leather conditioning for a fresh cabin.

Tar Remover

Expert removal of tough tar spots, road paint, and industrial fallout from your paintwork, preparing it for the next step.

Headlight Restoration

Restores clarity to foggy or yellowed headlights, improving nighttime visibility and the overall look of your vehicle.

Bike Detailing & Polishing

Specialized detailing and polishing for motorcycles to enhance the shine of paint and chrome, bringing out the best in your two-wheeler.

Custom Painting

Personalize your ride. We offer expert painting for brake calipers, emblems, and other custom accents to make your vehicle stand out.

Window Tinting

Reduce heat and glare inside your vehicle with Ozon's professional window tinting and cooling film services.
