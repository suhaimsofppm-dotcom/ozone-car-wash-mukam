About Ozone Detailing & Car Wash

Welcome to Ozone Detailing and Car Wash, where passion for cars meets precision and care. Founded on the principle that every vehicle deserves to look its absolute best, we have become a trusted name for discerning car and bike owners.

Our mission is simple: to provide an unparalleled detailing experience that restores, protects, and enhances your vehicle's appearance. We use only industry-leading products and the latest techniques, from meticulous hand washes to advanced ceramic coatings. Our skilled technicians treat every vehicle as their own, ensuring a flawless finish and complete customer satisfaction every time.

Our Services

Car Wash

A thorough exterior hand wash and dry, leaving your vehicle spotless and shining from top to bottom.

Car/Bike Detailing

Our comprehensive detailing service restores your vehicle's look with paint correction, polishing, and waxing.

Foam Wash

Gentle yet effective foam cannon wash that lifts away dirt and grime without harming your car's paintwork.

Interior Cleaning

Deep cleaning of all interior surfaces, including vacuuming, upholstery shampooing, and leather conditioning.

Steam Wash

An eco-friendly, high-pressure steam clean that sanitizes surfaces and removes tough stains and odors.

Ceramic Coating

Apply a durable, hydrophobic layer of protection against scratches, UV rays, and contaminants for a lasting gloss.

Underbody Coating

Protect your vehicle's chassis from rust, corrosion, and road debris with our durable, high-performance undercoating treatment.

Custom Painting

Personalize your ride. We offer expert painting for brake calipers, emblems, and other custom accents to make your vehicle stand out.

Paint Protection Film (PPF)

Install a transparent, self-healing film to protect your car's paint from rock chips, scratches, and road debris.

Headlight Restoration

Restore clarity to foggy or yellowed headlights, improving visibility and the overall look of your vehicle.

Seat Cover Fitting

Professional installation of custom-fit seat covers to protect your original upholstery and enhance interior style.

Window Tinting

High-quality window films that provide privacy, reduce heat and glare, and protect against harmful UV rays.
